Our Journey

Crafting Happiness Since 2020

The Home Bakery began its delightful journey in 2020, founded from home by Mrs. Anjali Nilesh Pande out of her passion for baking and spreading happiness through homemade goodness.

What started as a small kitchen experiment soon turned into a beloved local bakery, known for its authentic flavors, premium ingredients, and heartfelt service.

Today, we continue to bake with the same love and dedication, creating sweet memories for every celebration and everyday moment.

Meet Our Baker
